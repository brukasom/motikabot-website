# Privacy Policy — MotiCart

**Last updated:** July 5, 2026

## 1. Who we are

MotiCart is a shopping list and grocery list app provided by an individual developer operating under the developer name **MotikaBot**.

For privacy questions, data requests, or account/data deletion help, you can contact us at:

**Email:** motikabot@gmail.com

The controller for personal data processed through MotiCart is the individual developer operating under the developer name **MotikaBot**.

## 2. What information MotiCart processes

MotiCart is designed as an **offline-first** app.

If you use MotiCart only for local lists, your shopping lists, item names, quantities, units, checked/unchecked states, archives, and app settings are stored locally on your device. Local-only list content is not sent to MotiCart servers unless you use features that require cloud synchronization, such as shared or family lists.

If you use account, shared, or family features, MotiCart processes the information needed to create your account and synchronize shared lists. This may include:

- email address;
- display name;
- profile photo URL;
- user account identifier;
- shared group membership;
- group ownership status;
- invite information;
- shared list names;
- item names;
- quantities;
- units;
- bought/unbought status;
- archive or history information;
- technical metadata needed to keep shared lists synchronized.

Shared-list data is processed using Supabase services for authentication, database storage, realtime synchronization, and backend functions.

When you sign in, MotiCart uses Google Sign-In through Android Credential Manager. MotiCart uses Google account information such as your email address, display name, and profile photo URL to create and maintain your MotiCart account and display your profile in shared spaces.

If you purchase a subscription, purchases are handled through Google Play Billing and subscription access is managed through RevenueCat. MotiCart may process subscription-related information such as product ID, purchase status, entitlement status, plan type, purchase token, or transaction reference in order to determine whether Plus or Family features are active.

MotiCart does not collect or store your payment card number, bank account number, or full payment details.

MotiCart may also process technical data through third-party SDKs:

- **Google AdMob** may process advertising-related and device-related information when ads are requested or displayed in the free version.
- **Google UMP SDK** may be used to manage privacy choices and consent messages related to advertising.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** may process crash reports, device information, app version information, installation identifiers, stack traces, and technical diagnostic data.

We do not intentionally include email addresses, authentication tokens, or shopping-list content in crash reports. Crash reports may still contain technical metadata generated by the app, device, or operating system when an error occurs.

## 3. Information received from other users

If another user invites you to a shared or family list, MotiCart may receive your email address or invitation-related metadata from that user.

We use this information only to create, send, manage, secure, and process the invitation and related shared-list access.

## 7. Ads and consent

MotiCart Free may display ads.

If ads are requested or shown, Google advertising technologies may process information such as advertising identifiers, device information, IP-related information, app interaction signals, and ad performance data.

Where applicable law requires consent for advertising-related processing or access to information on your device, MotiCart may request that consent through Google’s consent tools before enabling non-essential advertising operations.

If you decline consent, MotiCart may still be usable, but advertising functionality or ad personalization may be limited.

You may be able to change advertising-related privacy choices through in-app privacy options where available, Android device settings, or Google account settings.

Paid plans are intended to provide an ad-free in-app experience.

## 8. Subscriptions and billing

Subscription purchases are processed through Google Play Billing.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play.

MotiCart does not collect or store full payment card numbers, bank account numbers, or other full payment instrument details.

Some transaction, billing, tax, fraud-prevention, audit, or dispute-resolution records may be retained by Google Play, RevenueCat, or other relevant providers where required or permitted by law.

## 9. Data retention and deletion

If you use only local lists, your local list data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, uninstall the app, or use MotiCart deletion or cleanup features.

If you use an account or shared features, account data and synchronized shared-list data are kept for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain your account, operate shared groups, resolve issues, prevent abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

MotiCart provides in-app account deletion.

When you delete your account, MotiCart starts deletion of your app account and associated cloud data, signs you out locally, and may delete local app data such as local databases, preferences, cached files, and app settings.

If you delete your account while you are part of a shared group owned by another user, some shared-list records may need to remain in adjusted form so the list can continue working for the remaining members. In that case, MotiCart may remove your membership references and preserve or reassign certain technical references needed to maintain the shared list.

Some data may remain after deletion where retention is required or permitted for legal, tax, accounting, billing, audit, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, or security reasons.
