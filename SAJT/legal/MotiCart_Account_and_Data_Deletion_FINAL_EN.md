# Account Deletion and Data Deletion — MotiCart

**Last updated:** July 5, 2026

This page explains how users can delete their MotiCart account and request deletion of app-related data.

**App name:** MotiCart  
**Developer:** MotikaBot  
**Package name:** com.motikabot.moticart  
**Contact email:** motikabot@gmail.com

## 1. Delete Your Account in the App

If you are signed in to MotiCart, you can delete your account directly inside the app:

1. Open MotiCart.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Choose **Delete all data**.
4. Confirm the deletion request.

You must be online and signed in for in-app account deletion to complete.

## 2. Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email.

Send your request to:

**motikabot@gmail.com**

Please include:

- **Subject:** MotiCart account deletion request
- The email address used to sign in to MotiCart.
- A short message saying that you want your MotiCart account and associated app data deleted.

For security reasons, we may need to verify that you are the account owner before completing the request.

Deletion requests should normally be sent from the same email address used to sign in to MotiCart. If you no longer have access to that email address, please explain the situation in your request. If we cannot reasonably verify that you are the account owner, we may be unable to complete the deletion request by email.

Email deletion requests can delete or remove account-related cloud data from our backend. They cannot directly remove local data stored only on your device. To remove local device data, you can delete lists inside the app, clear the app data from Android settings, or uninstall the app.

## 3. What Is Deleted

When you delete your MotiCart account, we delete or remove account-related data from our app backend, including:

- your MotiCart account profile;
- your email address and display name from our app backend;
- your MotiCart user ID from our app backend;
- shared or family lists that you own;
- shared list items, invite codes, archive history, and related records for groups you own;
- your membership references from shared groups owned by other users.

When account deletion is completed from inside the app, MotiCart is also designed to sign you out locally and wipe local app data stored by MotiCart on that device, such as the local database, app preferences, cached files, and local app storage.

## 4. Shared Groups Owned by Other Users

If you joined a shared or family list owned by another user, deleting your account does not automatically delete that owner’s shared group.

In that case, MotiCart removes your membership references and account identity from that group where possible, while preserving the group for the remaining members.

## 5. Local-Only Use Without an Account

If you use MotiCart without signing in, your local shopping lists are stored on your device.

Because no MotiCart cloud account exists in this case, there is no online account to delete. You can remove local-only data by deleting lists inside the app, clearing the app data from Android settings, or uninstalling the app.

## 6. Request Deletion of Other App Data

If you want to request deletion of app-related data without deleting your entire account, contact us at:

**motikabot@gmail.com**

Please include the email address used to sign in to MotiCart and describe what data you want deleted.

Some data may be necessary for your account or shared-list features to work. If deleting specific data would prevent a feature from working, we may explain the available options, such as deleting your account or leaving a shared group.

## 7. Subscription and Billing Records

Deleting your MotiCart account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription.

If you have an active Plus or Family subscription, you should cancel it through your Google Play account settings to stop future renewals.

Uninstalling MotiCart does not automatically cancel your subscription.

MotiCart does not store your credit card number, bank account details, or full payment method information. Payments are handled by Google Play.

Some billing, transaction, tax, fraud-prevention, refund, dispute, or legal compliance records may remain with Google Play, RevenueCat, or other payment-related service providers according to their own legal and operational requirements.

## 8. Data That May Be Retained for a Limited Time

Some technical records may remain for a limited period where needed for security, fraud prevention, crash diagnostics, legal compliance, billing, dispute handling, or backup integrity.

For example, crash reports and diagnostic records may be retained by Firebase Crashlytics for a limited period according to its retention practices.

We do not intentionally send shopping list contents, email addresses, authentication tokens, or payment card details in crash reports.

## 9. Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible after we receive and verify the request.

Some requests may take longer if verification is required, if the request is unclear, or if certain data must be retained for legal, security, billing, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, or platform compliance reasons.
